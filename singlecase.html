About the project:

In 2024, Deiz Car Rental launched in Dubai with a goal to stand out in the premium rental market. The client turned to us to shape their brand communication from the ground up.

We developed a tailored social media strategy, created engaging video content, and took over full SMM management. Our focus was on building trust, highlighting the service’s convenience, and showcasing the lifestyle behind the wheel — all through a strong, cohesive digital presence.

how We Did it:

We started developing the strategy with in-depth research of the Dubai market and audience. During customer development interviews, we noticed the type of sensual women coming to dance at Deiz Car Rental. They value the sensations during dance and live life’s moments so vividly that we couldn’t help but be inspired by them!

That’s how the brand’s global message "Feel your mooves" came to life. It unites the passion for dancing, the sensuality of the women ("feel"), and the dance movements at Deiz Car Rental ("mooves"). The positioning is just one part of the school’s brand platform, which also includes the brand pyramid, tone of voice, unique selling proposition, and audience insights.

We developed a new visual concept, with the signature pink color as its main focus. The brand identity also reflects the feelings and emotions experienced after a class at the studio.

Based on the strategy, we created creative content pillars for the brand’s social media. We took trends into account, such as nostalgia, UGC, ballet core, and more. See for yourself the stylish content we’re constantly creating for Deiz Car Rental!
